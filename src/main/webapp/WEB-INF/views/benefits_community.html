Benefits to Community Partners

The amateur sport associations will receive funding to support their activities, teams, and individual athletes within their associations. In essence, they will have a group of corporate sponsors that they can depend upon for funding on an ongoing basis - without the hassle of knocking on doors for sponsorship.

The non-profit and charity organizations will receive funding to support the charitable work they do within our communities. Organizations such as the Lions Clubs, MADD, Boys and Girls Clubs, local fire departments, schools, churches and many others do tremendous work in our province. Communiy Game Changer will help them increase their financial position and be able to continue to provide vital services back to the residents of Prince Edward Island.

Benefits

  • Ongoing funding

  • Better financial position

  • Less stress on volunteers

  • Promote within membership

  • Less fundraising

  • Enable family with financial limitations to participate
