Benefits to Families

You benefit because you simply buy the products that you always buy, and you get a rebate for doing so! Community Game Changer will send the funds generated by your rebate directly to the teams or organizations that your membership account dictates. If you chose to receive a portion or all of the rebate personality Community Game Changer will send an e-Transfer on a quarterly bases or a check annually. It's easy and it works!

Simply register with Community Game Changer and the change will begin! Once registered, all you have to do is present your card (name or membership number) at participating merchants, and the rest is taken care of on your behalf.

Members of Community Game Changer will purchase everyday items such as gas, pizza, sports equipment, coffee, furniture and construction materials through companies that have agreed to help sponsor our families costs in supporting charities, non-profits, sports teams and individuals with health issues.

Our family members will have ongoing support from these merchants and the only ask from our merchant partners is to purchase items that you would normally be buying anyways...

Benefits

  • Your family benefits through quarterly e-Transfers or yearly checks

  • Support charities and organizations that you have an emotional attachment too

  • Support local business

  • Reduce registration fees

  • Ability to help relatives and friends with costs of participating in sports

  • Benefit other Families with financial limitations
