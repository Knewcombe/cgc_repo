Benefits to Merchants

The merchants will have a built in buying group that has an emotional attachment to the athletes, charities and non-profit's that are going to benefit from the financial contributions made by the merchants. The merchants will also be able to see an immediate return on investment, because contributions are only passed on to the associations after actual purchases have been made at participating businesses. The results of the program are measurable, unlike so many other advertising activities or donations. Participation in the Community Game Changer Program also gives the participating merchants competitive advantage over their competition, and enhances customer loyalty and retention.

Benefits

  • Give back to the community groups

  • Create a buying group with an emotional attachment

  • Ongoing relationships with young demographic

  • Loyalty Program

  • Support only after purchases have been made

  • Community goodwill

  • Data regarding your clientele

  • Your one and only donation program
